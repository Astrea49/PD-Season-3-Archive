A lazy river - how fun~ It's pretty simple, really. There are multiple places where anyone can simply enter into the river - yes, the slide mentioned in #the-slides is one such place, but there's also many other places that have tubes and a loading station, allowing you to quickly get into the river. The river itself is calm and goes around the entire place. The water itself is rather warm, and while there's a constant, unbreakable flow to this place, it's also a very slow flow that's easily swimmable out of. The water itself also isn't that deep, being less than 3 feet/1 meter. There are multiple ladders that you can use to exit, or you can just wait till you reach the next loading station and move yourself to get off there. Otherwise, just sit back and relax!
