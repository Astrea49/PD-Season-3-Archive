The Killing Game There's no point in going over each and every rule of this, I believe. Why, that would be a waste of paper - a tree was sacrifice to make this diary, so I will honour it. Instead, to jot down what I know... this is a game where a group of people are trapped in a location - and the only way to clutch the sweet relief of escape is to kill another human being. It's a distasteful game, but that goes without saying. Talia Aelius seems to be the one in charge, at least as far as I can see... oh, and there's her servant boy. I feel for the poor soul, truly... but, anyways. If I'm to take Miss Aelius at her word, then Hope's Peak Academy is not related to this game? That's certainly a relief, but that begs the question of how they got a hold of us. Was the entire letter a lie from the beginning, or were we intercepted from Hope's Peak itself? What is particularly fascinating, though, is that someone seems to have tried to save us. That person? Mayumi Takimura - I believe she is tied to Takimura Technologies, given the name... though I'm not an expert on either. But alas... someone out there knew of this Killing Game and tried to put a stop to it. That's important... perhaps there are more who wish to do the same?
Us/'Participants' Of course, no play is complete without the participants... and whether we're willing or not, we're to serve as the actors on this twisted stage. There are twenty of us in total, I believe. As of today (Day 1), I only properly know four people... it tugs at my heartstrings, truly! I'd love to get to know the others - most preferably in a less dire context. But, beggars cannot be choosers. We must play with the hand fate has dealt us, I suppose.
Fusanosuke Fukushima Fufu... there's a lot I could say about myself... but then, if some scoundrel were to pinch my diary - then all my dirty secrets - they would be exposed!
